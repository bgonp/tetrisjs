Place tetraminos on the board to make full lines. You play in two boards and you can switch between them whenever you want by pressing 'c'.

The more lines you make at once, the more points you earn:
- 1 line: 100 points
- 2 lines: 800 points
- 3 lines: 2700 points
- 4 lines: 6400 points
Win small extra points by lower a tetramino pressing 'space'.

Earn points multiplier by swithcing between boards. If you clear a line and the previous cleared one was in other board, then the multiplier will be incremented by 0.25

CONTROLS

Left / Right arrows
Move blocks left /right

Down arrow
Move blocks fast down

Space
Translate blocks to the bottom

Up Arrow
Rotate blocks

C key
Switch between boards

Escape
Pause the game

S key
Start a new game
