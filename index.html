You will play in two boards and you have two different modes:
- Auto-switch: Everytime you clear a row the boards switch.
- Manual: You can switch between boards whenever you want by pressing 'c'.

The more lines you make at once, the more points you earn:
- 1 line: 100 points
- 2 lines: 800 points
- 3 lines: 2700 points
- 4 lines: 6400 points
Win small extra points by lower a tetrimino by pressing 'space'.

In manual mode you can get score multipliers by clearing rows alternatively between boards.

CONTROLS

Left / Right arrows
Move blocks left /right

Down arrow
Move blocks fast down

Space
Translate blocks to the bottom

Up Arrow
Rotate blocks

C key
Switch between boards (only manual mode)

Escape
Pause the game

S key
Start a new game

SETTINGS

Create your custom tetriminos
(min 2 blocks)
